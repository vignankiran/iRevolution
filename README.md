# iRevolution: iPhone Impact in India
A Data-driven Exploration of Apple's iPhone Impact in India using Tableau
📱 iRevolution: A Data-Driven Exploration of Apple's iPhone Impact in India
📌 Project Overview

iRevolution is a data analytics project that explores the growth, market influence, and consumer impact of Apple's iPhone in India. Using data visualization and business intelligence techniques, the project analyzes sales trends, product performance, pricing patterns, and market dynamics to uncover insights into Apple's expanding presence in the Indian smartphone market.

The project was developed using Tableau and leverages datasets containing information about Apple products, sales performance, market statistics, and customer trends.

#🎯 Objectives
Analyze Apple's iPhone market performance in India.
Identify sales trends across different iPhone generations.
Study pricing patterns and product positioning.
Explore factors influencing consumer adoption.
Create interactive dashboards for data-driven decision-making.
